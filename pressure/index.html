How many PSI are in 1 bar?▶

Exactly 1 bar is equal to 14.5038 PSI. Conversely, 1 PSI is approximately 0.0689 bar. Bar is a metric unit of pressure used globally, while PSI (pounds per square inch) is the primary unit in North America.
