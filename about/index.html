The Engineering Behind TradeConvert

Built for professionals who need accuracy without the clutter of consumer-grade converters.

Our Mission

TradeConvert was born from a simple observation: most online unit converters are designed for general use, often lacking the precision and context required for professional trade work. Whether you're calculating circuit breaker sizing or concrete volume, a rounding error isn't just a number—it's a material cost or a safety hazard.

The "Engineering Book" Aesthetic

We believe that professional tools should feel professional. Our design is inspired by classic engineering handbooks and field manuals—prioritizing legibility, structural hierarchy, and authority. No flashing ads, no unnecessary animations—just the data you need.

Verified Standards

All our conversion factors are sourced directly from NIST (National Institute of Standards and Technology) and NEC (National Electrical Code) publications. We provide the formulas alongside the results so you can verify the math for your own submittals.
