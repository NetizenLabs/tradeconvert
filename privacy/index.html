Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Here is how we handle your data.

1. Data Collection

TradeConvert is designed as a client-side tool. We do not store or transmit the values you enter into our calculators to any server. All calculations are performed locally in your browser.

2. Cookies

We use minimal cookies for site performance and basic analytics to understand how many people use our tools. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use Google Fonts to provide our classic engineering aesthetic. Google Fonts may collect your IP address to deliver font files to your browser.
