Lumber Calculator — Board Feet & Dimension Guide

Calculate board footage for hardwood ordering and framing estimates. Reference nominal versus actual dimensions for softwood lumber.

A Board Foot (BF) is a unit of volume equal to a board 12" wide, 12" long, and 1" thick (144 cubic inches). Formula: (Thickness" × Width" × Length') / 12. Note that hardwood is often sold by "quarters" (e.g., 4/4 is 1" thick).

Hardwood "Quarters"

Hardwood thickness is measured in quarters of an inch:

  • 4/4 = 1 inch
  • 5/4 = 1.25 inches
  • 8/4 = 2 inches
