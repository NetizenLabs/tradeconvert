Using a Shower Tile Calculator

A reliable shower tile calculator is critical because bathrooms are tight spaces requiring precise cuts around plumbing.

  • Shower Wall Tile Calculator: When calculating wall tile, remember to measure the total height you intend to tile up to. Standard builder-grade showers stop at 72 inches (6 feet), but premium custom showers often tile all the way to the 8-foot ceiling or even across the ceiling itself!
  • Diagonal Patterns: If you plan to lay your shower pan tile or wall tile on a 45-degree diagonal pattern, you must increase your waste factor to at least 20%. Diagonal layouts require cutting almost every single perimeter tile, significantly reducing the usable square footage of your material.
Code Reference

Ensure your waterproofing membrane behind the tile is completely seamless and watertight before adhering any wall tile.

Field Note Are you building custom soap niches or a shaving bench? These features require bullnose trim or Schluter edge profiles. Make sure you calculate the linear footage of all outside corners to order the correct amount of edge trim!
