Electrical Calculator — Watts to Amps & Wire Gauge Guide

Calculate electrical loads, determine circuit breaker requirements, and reference AWG wire sizes. All values based on NEC 2023 standards.

To calculate Amps from Watts: divide Watts by Volts (I = P / V). For example, a 2400W load on 240V draws 10A. NEC requires a 125% safety factor for continuous loads, meaning a 10A continuous load requires a 15A breaker (the 80% rule).

The 80% Rule

NEC 210.20(A) requires that continuous loads (on for 3+ hours) not exceed 80% of a breaker's rated ampacity.

Example: A 20A breaker can only supply 16A continuously.
