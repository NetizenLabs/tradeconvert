Standard Drywall Volume Formulas

This drywall calculator divides your structural space into geometric planes and deducts standard structural openings (like standard doors = 20 sq ft, windows = 15 sq ft):

Geometric Area Calculations:
Ceiling Area = Length × Width
Wall Area = 2 × (Length + Width) × Ceiling Height
Total Net Area = (Ceiling Area + Wall Area) - Deductible Openings
Required Sheets = Ceil( (Total Net Area × (1 + Waste%)) ÷ Sheet Coverage Area )

Waste Guidelines

Standard Wall Layout: Simple rectangular room with few windows requires exactly **10%** extra material.

Arched or Angled Walls: Complex drywall cuts create higher wastage. Add **15% to 20%** waste margin.

Field Note Always hang drywall horizontally on residential walls when possible. Horizontal boards span studs better and make seam taping infinitely easier at chest height!
